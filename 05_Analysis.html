Percentage of Agree

Here we compare our heuristics with the percentage of those who do not agree the government should redistribute out of those who agree inequality is too high (the ‘inconsistent’ attitude individuals). We also compare them based on the correlation because the inconsistent too much inequality perceivers do not pattern by two of three heuristics.
